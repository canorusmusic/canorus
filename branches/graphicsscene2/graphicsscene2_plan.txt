CADrawable inherits QGraphicsItemGroup
- every CADrawable has besides integrated childItems() its own variables for storing eg. staff lines, note heads/stem, barline line etc.
- CADrawableStaff also has sharedElements list of CADrawableMusElements sorted by xPos
- CADrawableStaff also has voiceElements list of CADrawablePlayableElements per voice sorted by xPos
